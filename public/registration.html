Author Guidelines
  • All accepted, registered and presented papers in ICCCEM 2022 will be published in Elsevier and SSRN Proceedings.
  • Registration fees cover Elsevier SSRN: Proceedings publication and Certificate (Soft Copy) only.
  • The conference will be held in Offline mode. Authors can present their paper through offline/online mode.
  • One registration fee will cover one paper only. If an author has got more than one accepted paper, each paper has also to be registered separately.
  • Registration fee to be paid only if the author receives an "Acceptance email" after the peer review process/publisher.
  • Please fill the Registration form available online after receiving intimation regarding the acceptance of your paper.
  • Registration Fee to be paid ASAP within the deadline.
Plagiarism, Refund And Cancellation Policy
  • 50% of registration fee can be refunded for cancellation made in writing and received by the ICCCEM Chair on or before 1st December 2022. After refund and cancellation, the corresponding final paper will be excluded in the proceeding. Refunds (if any) will be made after the conference by online transfer.
  • There will be no refund for cancellation made after 1st December 2022.
  • The ICCCEM 2022 takes very much care while evaluating the technical content of the paper before the acceptance. Plagiarism is the sole responsibility of authors if it is found in the Camera Ready Paper submitted by the authors. There will be no refund, if the paper is later rejected by the publisher citing the similarity / Plagiarism score. The conference is not responsible for such cases.
